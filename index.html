Obesity levels in individuals from Mexico, Peru and Colombia (2019)

This data contains estimations of obesity levels in people from the countries of Mexico, Peru and Columbia with ages between 14 and 61. Obesity levels for survey data are based on WHO.

Here we explore habits of exercise, age, and use of technology devices such as computers and smartphones.

Some argue that these factors play a role in obesity. For instance, exercise helps fight obesity. As people get older they are more likely to be overweight. Using technology makes people more sedentary, less physically active, and thus more overweight.
