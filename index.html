Obesity levels in individuals from Mexico, Peru and Colombia

This data contains estimations of obesity levels in people from the countries of Mexico, Peru and Columbia with ages between 14 and 61 taken in 2019. Obesity levels for survey data are based on WHO. The amount of subjects are normally distributed across all obesity levels.

Some argue that there are factors which play a role in obesity. For instance, exercise helps fight obesity. As people get older they are more likely to be overweight. Using technology makes people more sedentary, less physically active, and thus more overweight.

Here we explore habits of exercise, age, and use of technology devices such as computers and smartphones and find that it is not necessarily the case for all three of these factors.
